Product Manager - Independent Project

Personyfi is where mail merge meets mobile. This idea stemmed from wasting time over the holidays to send out personalized messages to friends and loved ones. Personyfi fixes this problem through our slick and simple texting process. First, select the contacts you want to send a message to. Second, type out your personalized message (insert the first name variable where it fits). Third, confirm all changes or make a few individual edits for further personalization. Lastly, watch your messages get sent off in real time.

The number of usecases for this product is far beyond my vision, but we hope to continue honing our product and satisfying our customers.
